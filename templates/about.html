Our Story

Founded in 2025, Turf PlayGrounds Booking emerged from a simple idea: make sports accessible to everyone by connecting players with quality turf facilities.

We noticed that finding and booking sports turfs was often complicated, time-consuming, and unreliable. Players struggled to find available slots, while turf owners missed potential bookings due to poor visibility.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of sports enthusiasts across India, providing a seamless platform that benefits both players and facility owners.
